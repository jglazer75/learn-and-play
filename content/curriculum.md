# **Project: "The Player 1 & Player 2 Chronicles"**

**A 50-Week Interactive Curriculum for Educational Gaming**

**Participants:**

* **Guide (JMG):** Video Game Historian / Strategist  
* **Apprentice (ACG):** The Builder / Pilot (Age 7\)

## **Part 1: The Curriculum**

*Weekly Class Time: Sundays, 2–3 Hours*

### **Block 1: The Arcade Roots & The 8-Bit Era (Weeks 1–10)**

*Objective: Joystick mastery, 2-button coordination, and understanding "High Scores."*

* **Week 1: The Maze Chase**  
  * **Game:** *Pac-Man* (Retro Handheld/Arcade)  
  * **Activity:** "Pass and Play" – alternate turns upon death.  
  * **Objective:** D-Pad cardinal directions (Up, Down, Left, Right).  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The birth of the "Mascot" character.  
* **Week 2: The First Jump**  
  * **Game:** *Donkey Kong* (Arcade Version on Switch/RH)  
  * **Activity:** Reach the top of Level 1\.  
  * **Objective:** Timing the "Jump" button.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The debut of Mario (Jumpman).  
* **Week 3: Space Defense**  
  * **Game:** *Galaga* (Retro Handheld)  
  * **Activity:** Get the "Dual Fighter" power-up.  
  * **Objective:** Risk vs. Reward (sacrificing a ship to get double power).  
  * **Historian Lesson:** High Scores and the Golden Age of Arcades.  
* **Week 4: The Forgiving Platformer**  
  * **Game:** *Kirby’s Adventure* (NES/Switch Online)  
  * **Activity:** Beat World 1\.  
  * **Objective:** Recovery (flying back after a mistake) reduces frustration.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Accessible game design.  
* **Week 5: The "Audiobook" Adventure**  
  * **Game:** *The Secret of Monkey Island: Special Edition* (PC)  
  * **Activity:** Co-Pilot Mode. Dad reads/clicks; ACG solves puzzles.  
  * **Objective:** Logic and listening comprehension.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Point-and-Click Adventures (Sierra/LucasArts era).  
* **Week 6: Momentum & Physics**  
  * **Game:** *Sonic the Hedgehog 2* (Genesis/Switch)  
  * **Activity:** Dad plays Sonic, ACG plays Tails (Invincible Co-op).  
  * **Objective:** Reacting to high speeds without penalty.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The "Console Wars" (Nintendo vs. Sega).  
* **Week 7: The Beat-’Em-Up**  
  * **Game:** *TMNT: Shredder's Revenge* (Switch/PC)  
  * **Activity:** Co-op Story Mode (Easy).  
  * **Objective:** Button mashing vs. timed attacks; stress relief.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Arcade multiplayer cabinets.  
* **Week 8: Introduction to RPGs**  
  * **Game:** *Pokémon Let's Go, Pikachu\!* (Switch)  
  * **Activity:** Catching tutorial and first trainer battle.  
  * **Objective:** Turn-based logic (I wait, you go) & reading stats.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The "Pokémania" phenomenon.  
* **Week 9: Racing Basics**  
  * **Game:** *Mario Kart 8 Deluxe* (Switch)  
  * **Activity:** 50cc Cup with "Auto-Steer" OFF (if capable) or ON.  
  * **Objective:** Steering finesse (tilting stick vs. holding it).  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Mode 7 graphics and the evolution of racing.  
* **Week 10: Block 1 Review**  
  * **Game:** Revisit the favorite game from Weeks 1–9.  
  * **Activity:** Try to beat the previous high score.  
  * **Objective:** Reinforce mastery and confidence.

### **Block 2: The 16-Bit Golden Age & Cooperative Play (Weeks 11–20)**

*Objective: Shoulder buttons, complex maps, and working together.*

* **Week 11: The Perfect Platformer**  
  * **Game:** *Super Mario World* (SNES/Switch Online)  
  * **Activity:** Unlock Yoshi.  
  * **Objective:** Managing an inventory item (holding a shell/item).  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The launch of the SNES.  
* **Week 12: The Dungeon Crawler**  
  * **Game:** *The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening* (Switch Remake)  
  * **Activity:** The First Dungeon (Tail Cave).  
  * **Objective:** Map reading and spatial memory.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Action-Adventures vs. RPGs.  
* **Week 13: The Racing Sim (Lite)**  
  * **Game:** *Forza Horizon 4 or 5* (PC/Steam \- with controller)  
  * **Activity:** Free roam driving in a truck/cool car.  
  * **Objective:** Analog throttle control (gentle gas vs. floor it).  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Realistic physics vs. Arcade physics.  
* **Week 14: Puzzle Logic**  
  * **Game:** *Tetris Effect* (Switch/PC) or *Dr. Mario* (NES)  
  * **Activity:** Co-op mode or taking turns.  
  * **Objective:** Pattern matching and thinking ahead.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The Game Boy and mobile gaming origins.  
* **Week 15: Cooperative Chaos**  
  * **Game:** *Overcooked\! All You Can Eat* (Switch)  
  * **Activity:** Play the first few kitchens.  
  * **Objective:** Communication ("Pass me the tomato\!").  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The rise of "Couch Co-op" indie games.  
* **Week 16: The Interactive Cartoon**  
  * **Game:** *Cuphead* (Switch/PC) \- *Warning: Difficult\!*  
  * **Activity:** Play on "Simple" mode together.  
  * **Objective:** Appreciating art style (1930s animation). Failure is okay\!  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Hand-drawn animation in games.  
* **Week 17: Strategy Origins**  
  * **Game:** *SimCity* (SNES Version on Switch/Retro Handheld)  
  * **Activity:** Build a small town. Reach 10,000 population.  
  * **Objective:** System management (Power \+ Roads \+ Zones).  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Will Wright and the "Software Toy."  
* **Week 18: The Flight Simulator**  
  * **Game:** *Star Fox 64* (Switch Online)  
  * **Activity:** Complete the first level (Corneria).  
  * **Objective:** Inverted controls check (Up is Down) \- optional.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The Rumble Pak and haptic feedback.  
* **Week 19: The "Metroidvania"**  
  * **Game:** *Super Metroid* (SNES) or *Metroid Prime Remastered* (Switch)  
  * **Activity:** Map exploration; finding the Morph Ball.  
  * **Objective:** Backtracking and memory ("I can open that door now\!").  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Non-linear storytelling.  
* **Week 20: Block 2 Review**  
  * **Game:** Player's Choice.  
  * **Activity:** Draw a map of the favorite game world together on paper.

### **Block 3: The 3D Revolution (Weeks 21–30)**

*Objective: Camera control (dual analog sticks) and 3D space.*

* **Week 21: The 3D Playground**  
  * **Game:** *Super Mario Odyssey* (Switch) or *Astro's Playroom* (if PS5 available)  
  * **Activity:** Just running around the first kingdom.  
  * **Objective:** Moving the camera while moving the character (The hardest skill\!).  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The transition from 2D sprites to 3D polygons (Mario 64).  
* **Week 22: The 3D Collector**  
  * **Game:** *Spyro Reignited Trilogy* (Switch/PC)  
  * **Activity:** Clear the first hub world.  
  * **Objective:** Exploring every nook and cranny.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The PlayStation era mascot wars.  
* **Week 23: Action Strategy**  
  * **Game:** *Pikmin 3 Deluxe* or *Pikmin 4* (Switch)  
  * **Activity:** Day 1 & 2\. Controlling the squad.  
  * **Objective:** Multitasking and time management.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Shigeru Miyamoto’s gardening hobby inspiration.  
* **Week 24: Co-op 3D Puzzles**  
  * **Game:** *Luigi's Mansion 3* (Switch)  
  * **Activity:** Unlock Gooigi (Player 2).  
  * **Objective:** Asymmetrical puzzle solving.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Physics engines in games (sucking up curtains/objects).  
* **Week 25: The Rhythm Game**  
  * **Game:** *Taiko no Tatsujin* (Switch) or *Beat Saber* (if VR) or *Just Dance*  
  * **Activity:** Play 3 songs.  
  * **Objective:** Rhythm and full-body/hand coordination.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The Arcade rhythm game boom (DDR).  
* **Week 26: The "Tactics" Game**  
  * **Game:** *Mario \+ Rabbids Kingdom Battle* (Switch)  
  * **Activity:** First 3 battles.  
  * **Objective:** Turn-based strategy on a grid (Intro to Dad’s Civ/Strategy love).  
  * **Historian Lesson:** XCOM and the Strategy Genre.  
* **Week 27: The Sandbox**  
  * **Game:** *Minecraft* (Creative Mode)  
  * **Activity:** "Build Our House." No monsters, just blocks.  
  * **Objective:** 3D spatial creativity.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The Indie Revolution and "Early Access."  
* **Week 28: Physics & Comedy**  
  * **Game:** *Untitled Goose Game* (Switch/PC)  
  * **Activity:** Complete the Garden to-do list together.  
  * **Objective:** Stealth and manipulating physics objects.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Viral games and internet culture.  
* **Week 29: The Open Road**  
  * **Game:** *Burnout Paradise Remastered* (Switch/PC)  
  * **Activity:** Just drive. Crash into things.  
  * **Objective:** High-speed reaction times.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Open World driving games.  
* **Week 30: Block 3 Review**  
  * **Activity:** "The 3D Test." Play *Super Mario 64* (Switch Online) to see how hard the old camera was compared to modern games.

### **Block 4: Simulation, Logic & Building (Weeks 31–40)**

*Objective: Planning, logic, and creative agency.*

* **Week 31: Survival Lite**  
  * **Game:** *Minecraft* (Survival Mode \- Easy)  
  * **Activity:** Survive the first night. Build a shelter. Craft a pickaxe.  
  * **Objective:** Resource management.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Emergent gameplay (stories you create yourself).  
* **Week 32: Engineering 101**  
  * **Game:** *Poly Bridge* (Switch/PC) or *World of Goo*  
  * **Activity:** Build a bridge that doesn't break.  
  * **Objective:** Physics logic (Triangles are strong\!).  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Physics puzzle games.  
* **Week 33: The Farming Sim**  
  * **Game:** *Stardew Valley* (Switch/PC \- Co-op)  
  * **Activity:** Plant the first harvest.  
  * **Objective:** Daily routine and patience.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** *Harvest Moon* and the "Cozy Game" genre.  
* **Week 34: The God Game**  
  * **Game:** *Civilization Revolution* (Console \- Xbox backward compat if avail) OR *Civilization VI* (Switch \- Tutorial only)  
  * **Activity:** Found a city, build a warrior.  
  * **Objective:** The concept of "Turns" representing years.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Sid Meier and the 4X genre (Explore, Expand, Exploit, Exterminate).  
* **Week 35: The Graphic Adventure**  
  * **Game:** *Grim Fandango Remastered* (Switch/PC)  
  * **Activity:** "Audiobook Mode" (Dad plays/reads).  
  * **Objective:** Narrative appreciation and "Film Noir" style.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The death and rebirth of adventure games.  
* **Week 36: Programming Logic**  
  * **Game:** *Baba Is You* (Switch/PC)  
  * **Activity:** First 5 levels.  
  * **Objective:** "Rule breaking" logic (Flag is Win).  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Logic programming in games.  
* **Week 37: The Sport Sim**  
  * **Game:** *Super Mega Baseball 4* (Switch/PC)  
  * **Activity:** Play a 3-inning exhibition game.  
  * **Objective:** Understanding rules of sport in a digital format.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Evolution of sports games (*Tecmo Bowl* to *Madden*).  
* **Week 38: The Infinite Runner**  
  * **Game:** *Bit.Trip Runner* (Switch)  
  * **Activity:** Rhythm platforming.  
  * **Objective:** "Flow state" gaming.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Mobile games crossing to console.  
* **Week 39: The Modern Retro**  
  * **Game:** *Shovel Knight* (Switch/PC)  
  * **Activity:** First level (Pridemoor Keep).  
  * **Objective:** Comparing this to Week 4 (NES) \- how it feels better/modern.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Kickstarter and crowd-funded games.  
* **Week 40: Block 4 Review**  
  * **Activity:** Build a "Monument to Gaming" in your shared Minecraft world.

### **Block 5: Modern Mastery & Graduation (Weeks 41–50)**

*Objective: Full competency, complex controls, and graduation.*

* **Week 41: Advanced Co-op**  
  * **Game:** *Portal 2* (Switch/PC)  
  * **Activity:** Co-op Campaign.  
  * **Objective:** Dual-stick movement \+ Logic \+ Physics.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Valve and environmental storytelling.  
* **Week 42: The RPG Journey**  
  * **Game:** *Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door* (Switch)  
  * **Activity:** The Prologue.  
  * **Objective:** Reading, math (damage calculation), and story.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** JRPGs turning into Action RPGs.  
* **Week 43: Exploration**  
  * **Game:** *A Short Hike* (Switch/PC)  
  * **Activity:** Reach the top of the mountain.  
  * **Objective:** Peaceful exploration.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Games as emotional experiences.  
* **Week 44: The Modern Shooter (Kid Friendly)**  
  * **Game:** *Splatoon 3* (Switch)  
  * **Activity:** Story Mode (Single player) or Turf War.  
  * **Objective:** Gyro aiming controls.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Nintendo's take on the shooter genre.  
* **Week 45: History of the Future**  
  * **Game:** *No Man's Sky* (PC/Switch \- Creative Mode)  
  * **Activity:** Fly from a planet to space.  
  * **Objective:** Scale and procedural generation.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Procedural generation (how computers build worlds).  
* **Week 46: The Roguelike**  
  * **Game:** *Vampire Survivors* (Switch/PC \- Co-op)  
  * **Activity:** Survive 15 minutes.  
  * **Objective:** Auto-battlers and "builds."  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The "Rogue" lineage and addiction loops.  
* **Week 47: The Fighting Game**  
  * **Game:** *Super Smash Bros. Ultimate* (Switch)  
  * **Activity:** 1v1 Duel (Dad vs. ACG). Items ON.  
  * **Objective:** Reaction speed and knowing your character.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** The Museum of Gaming (Smash Bros as a history lesson).  
* **Week 48: The Final Project (Design)**  
  * **Game:** *Super Mario Maker 2* (Switch)  
  * **Activity:** ACG builds a level for Dad to beat.  
  * **Objective:** Understanding game design from the creator's side.  
  * **Historian Lesson:** Level design.  
* **Week 49: The Final Project (Play)**  
  * **Game:** *Super Mario Maker 2* (Switch)  
  * **Activity:** Dad builds a level for ACG to beat.  
  * **Objective:** Testing skills learned over 50 weeks.  
* **Week 50: Graduation**  
  * **Activity:** Award Ceremony.  
  * **Task:** Visit a local arcade or game store to pick out *one* new game of his choice, fully informed by his taste.
